Seven Set Higher Secondary School (Secondary Section)

Seven Set School is an English medium co-educational institution. The school had its humble beginning with only Nursery classes at Upper Malki in the year 1967. The school was shifted to its present location in May 1974. Thence one new class was introduced every two or three years. Finally in the year 1987 the school was upgraded up to the tenth class with a first batch appearing in the H.S.L.C Examination. In 1991 Manish Gupta secured 1st Position in the General List. In the year 1998 the school annexed Class XI Arts followed by Class XII Arts in the year 1999 and in 2006 Science and Commerce streams were introduced. The school also celebrated its Silver Jubilee in the year 1993 and its Golden Jubilee in the year 2017.Seven Set School is an English medium co-educational institution. Our Motto

The Fear Of The Lord Is The Beginning Of Wisdom

Pledged on the Motto, the school also aims at preparing students to make a good living and teach them how to live a God-fearing life. The Bible says, ‘for what shall it profit a man if he gains the whole world and lose his soul”. Closer to God makes the difference. Hence, our pursuit for the personality development of a child is borne on intellectual, physical, moral and spiritual upbringing. These knit together becomes a force. For an overall smooth working of the institution we have framed rules as to minimize confusion and goals worked towards. May the Almighty give us the grace to be able to please Him who is so concerned about our whole being. We believe in humble submission to Him that life may be made worthy of living.

As the Principal of the school, it is an honour to welcome you to the website of Seven Set School, Shillong. Here at Seven Set, we strive to create a familial atmosphere where the children feel loved and recognized, a place where they not only learn from books but from experiences thus preparing them for the future where they are able to apply what they learn and share what they learn. We also strive to create an ambience where children develop responsibility, self control, good decision making skills, self confidence, determination and character building with high moral values. Like a compass, values tell us what direction to go and guide us in making decisions as well as to lead a God fearing life. Dear parents, your child’s learning involves and revolves around an effective partnership between home & school. The stronger the partnership, the more your child will benefit. We therefore look forward to your cooperation to attain our goals. Dear children, take charge of your happiness, surround yourself with wisdom and move ahead with determination to realize your vision.

Wishing you the best in life.

Mrs K.B Warjri, School Director

The attainment of wisdom and understanding is the aim of each and every person who seeks knowledge through education. It is with this objective that the school strives to build young minds that will have knowledge, character and skills that will enable them to be valuable members of the society. The school’s motto, “The fear of the Lord is the beginning of wisdom” aptly defines the core objective of Seven Set Hr Secondary School. Teaching the child to build life on strong values and a constant connection with God is as important as building a house on a strong foundation. As part of our endeavour, the academic activities and co curricular activities are tuned to bring out the best in each child and also to inculcate the best character that builds and prepare the child for future responsibilities. I therefore welcome all teachers, students and parents to utilise such activities of the school be it in campus or online through this website which will help our children realize, understand & explore their talents. “Alone we can do little but together we can do much”. On this note, together as parents, students and teachers, let us work as a team and complement each other’s efforts to achieve our dreams.

Dr (Mrs) G.Nongbri, Principal

Apply for Admission

Prescribed application forms for admission are issued from the school office at a specified time of the year, usually in August/ September. Parents/ Guardians are expected to fill up the form with utmost accuracy. Birth certificate, progress reports from the school last attended, immunization card, SC & S.T certificates (where applicable) and other certificates, if any should be submitted along with the application.
